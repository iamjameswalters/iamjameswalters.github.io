PyOhio 2024 Slides & Resources

By James Walters on Sat 27 July 2024.

Category: Python
Tags: Django, deployment, PyOhio, portfolio

Man, I love PyOhio! Last year was my first one, and I was so thrilled to hear that this year's event was going to be in person for the first time since COVID. I absolutely wanted to be a part of it, and I'm so glad I got to be! Thanks to you for listening to my talk! I hope it served you and helped you understand the deployment process for a Python web app a little better.

Below are links to any resources I mentioned, and a few that I didn't:
